Display Google Plus One (+1) Button to your Best Blog on  WordPress post / pages or as widget.

== Description ==

Add Google Plus One (+1) manually with shortcode `[ googleplusbutton ]` or automatically before or after the content of posts or pages.

Also you can display Google Plus One (+1) button as widget in your sidebar or other widget`s area.

== Installation ==

1. Upload to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Configure Settings
